DRUM NOTE MAP (each Drum track, 4 pages × 16 samples)
Each drum track has 4 pages of 16 samples.
Notes C0–D#1 (MIDI 0–27) trigger samples 1–16 on Page 1
Notes E1–G#2 (MIDI 28–44) trigger samples 1–16 on Page 2
Notes A2–C#4 (MIDI 45–61) trigger samples 1–16 on Page 3
Notes D4–F#5 (MIDI 62–78) trigger samples 1–16 on Page 4

Velocity controls loudness. NoteOn on channel 6–9 triggers the pad.
